Automate your world

ECE Pulse is an annual student-run conference held at Illinois that showcases innovation in electrical and computer engineering technologies. The conference is comprised of design competitions, hands-on workshops, tech talks, and networking sessions for students, alumni, faculty, and corporate representatives. Our aim is to inspire students by connecting them with cutting-edge technologies and innovative projects in the corporate world. This year's theme — Automate your World — will focus on showing attendees how our world is evolving to become more connected and collaborative everyday.
